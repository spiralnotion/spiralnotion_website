Search Engine Optimization

Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

What is SEO and why is it important?

SEO (Search engine optimization) is the process of making a web page easy to find, easy to crawl, and easy to categorize. It is about helping your potential customers find out about your business from other companies.

What are some fundamental elements that are in SEO?

  1. Backlinks
  2. Relevant, Optimized Contents
  3. Keywords
  4. User Experience
  5. Site Speed
  6. Mobile-Friendly

How can Spiral Notion help with your site’s SEO?

To create a SEO strategy that suits your business needs. We will first conduct your target market data through analytics reports, which includes keywords analysis, links, and existing users. Then perform a mobile-friendly audit to optimize user experiences. Lastly, we will generate site links that are relevant to your site, because your site should not only perform well in one search engine.
